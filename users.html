Who It's For

Built for the people
who run energy assets.

Whether you own, operate, finance, or build with energy assets — the gateway gives you the access and control you need.

Aggregators / VPP Operators

Need: more controllable MWs, faster

Connect batteries, solar, EV chargers, and flexible loads into one fleet with real-time telemetry and real-time dispatch. No custom OEM integrations. Implicit enrollment means assets are available the moment they connect.

BESS Financiers

Need: real-time asset visibility

See the health, utilization, and state of charge of every battery in your portfolio — in real time. Prove performance. Catch issues before they cost you.

OEMs

Need: standards compliance

Make your hardware interoperable and dispatch-ready without building a full software platform. Open standards, out of the box.

Utilities / DISCOMs

Need: more MWuM, less campaign effort

See behind-the-meter assets and dispatch flexible capacity in real time. Implicit enrollment means devices join DR programs automatically — no fragmented enrollment campaigns, higher participation rates, and more megawatts under management from day one.

Energy AI Platforms

Need: clean tools and context

Build optimization engines and AI agents on top of clean, standardized telemetry and dispatch APIs. No custom scraping, no unreliable OEM data.

Commercial Asset Owners

Need: savings and monetization

Turn your solar, batteries, and EV chargers into assets that actually work for you — lower bills, grid program revenue, and full visibility.

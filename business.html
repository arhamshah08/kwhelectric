Business Model

Hardware starts the relationship.
Software captures
the lifecycle value.

Start with the gateway. Add telemetry, dispatch, analytics, and AI control as your operations grow.

🛒

Hardware Sale

One-time sale or lease of the edge gateway to aggregators, asset owners, installers, or OEM partners. The gateway is the entry point into every site.

☁️

SaaS Platform Fee

Monthly software fee per device, site, MW, or portfolio for telemetry, dashboards, dispatch control, and analytics. Scales with the fleet.

📄

OEM Licensing

Embedded software or white-label gateway stack licensed to battery, inverter, EV charger, or smart device OEMs. Makes their hardware platform-ready from the factory.

📈

Aggregator / VPP Fee

Fee based on enrolled devices, controllable capacity, MW under management, or active dispatch availability. Aligns incentives with aggregator outcomes.

💸

Performance Revenue

Share of revenue from demand response, peak shaving, VPP dispatch, ancillary services, or market participation. We earn when assets earn.

🧠

Data + AI Services

Predictive maintenance, optimization, risk scoring, financing analytics, and AI dispatch tools built on top of the standardized data layer.
